Google OAuth flow

User hits "Sign in with Google"
First login → account created with status pending
Both admins get an email: "New access request from [name] — Approve / Reject"
Admin logs in → sees pending requests panel → approves and assigns role (calc-only or customer-access with specific customers selected)
User gets notified by email when approved
